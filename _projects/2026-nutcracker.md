(chrauf et al. Do capuchin monkeys use weight to select hammer tools, Anim Cogn 11, 413–422 (2008). https://doi.org/10.1007/s10071-007-0131-2) was used to determine the force needed to crack a macadamia nut.
https://www.topendsports.com/testing/norms/handgrip.html was used to determine average human grip strength to determine usability and human functionality.
